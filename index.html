Destination Overview

Our destination, Paris, is the capital of France and a city like no other. Known for its stunning architecture, art museums, and romantic atmosphere, Paris is a must-visit destination for any traveler.

Things to Do

From visiting the iconic Eiffel Tower to exploring the Louvre Museum, there's no shortage of things to do in Paris. Take a stroll along the Seine River, visit the Notre-Dame Cathedral, or enjoy a cabaret show at the Moulin Rouge.
